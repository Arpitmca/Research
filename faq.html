Frequently Asked Questions

We are here to answer your questions.

How i will get fund?

First you have to create your profile, then you will have a option to upload idea about your research, after that if someone is interested in your research can contact you.

Can we ask for research fund in science and technology field only?

No, you can upload about your research idea of any field, but your idea should be intresting so that it can attract investors.

Will i get fund for any research i want to do?

No, It depend on your idea that you will get fund or not, if it seems interesting to someone only then he will fund you.

Is there any terms and conditions on fund provided?

This depends on the investor as investor will directly contact you and negotiate about that, We only provide you a platform to showcase your research idea.

What is the maximum amount of fund i can get?

It depends on your investor and your idea, investor will communicate with you.

If i am unable to accomplish the task mentioned in research idea?

This is the matter between you and investor.
